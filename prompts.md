# prompts de partida (imagem + vídeo)

## âncora de estilo (cola em TODA geração de imagem)
> hand-drawn children's storybook illustration, aged warm yellowed paper, soft graphite pencil lines, slightly wavy/tremulous strokes, cross-hatch shading, muted washed-out colors (almost monochrome), vintage 1970s Brazilian educational cartoon vibe, transparent background, no text.

## por cena (imagem)
- **criança + páginas em branco:** a small child drawn in simple pencil strokes, standing before a path made of blank book pages stretching into the distance, wonder in the eyes.
- **TV antiga:** an old vintage tube television, light-letters-numbers-little-characters gently pouring out of the screen, hand-drawn.
- **celular (frio/confuso):** same child now still and dull, staring at a smartphone, cold bluish tones, fast chaotic notification shapes around, cluttered.
- **página rasgada (título):** a torn notebook page pinned over the scene, empty center for the title "O capítulo que falta".
- **páginas se preenchendo:** blank pages filling with tiny scenes — students studying, a teacher, a child reading, a family watching TV together, a lit-up community.

## vídeo (image-to-video, manter 2D)
> 2D hand-drawn frame-by-frame animation, subtle line boil, gentle idle motion, flat, NO 3D, NO camera realism, paper texture preserved, [descreve o micro-movimento: ex. "the pencil lines slowly draw themselves in", "child blinks and turns the page"].

**duração:** clipes curtos (3–6s) por cena, monta o resto no editor.
